What is the IC Comic Multiverse?

Here are Some Key Components.

Community Driven

IC comics is driven by the people, thier ideas, concepts, and voted upon decisions. Hopefully in the near future I can implement contribution awards. For now participation will be done through a DSCVR portal.

Go to DSCVR Portal

The Multiverse

The idea behind a multiverse is the ability to concieve unlimited worlds, characters, plots which are all interconnected. The project will start slowly and hopefully evolve in complexity as time goes on and people contribute to its development.

More Info Coming Soon

Chance driven

All actions taken within the multiverse are subject to chance aside from those voted by the community. I will be using a more simplified version of Dungeon crawler mechanics to dictate outcomes. Yes this means characters can die

Game Mechanics Alpha

NFT Comics

Iterations of characters and thier storylines will be minted and sold as issue NFTs in the near future. Hopefully with some help I can add some smart contract functionality so contributers like artists or other creators can get rewards.
